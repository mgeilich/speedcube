Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to SpeedCube AR. We value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how our application works. This policy outlines our commitment to your data security.

No Data Collection

SpeedCube AR is designed with a "privacy-first" architecture. We want to be very clear:

  • We do not collect any personal information.
  • We do not track your usage or behavior.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.
  • We do not store any of your cube scans on a server.

Camera Usage

The application requires access to your device's camera for the sole purpose of scanning your physical puzzle cube. This processing happens entirely on your device. No images or video streams are ever uploaded, transmitted, or stored outside of the temporary memory needed to identify the cube's state.

In-App Purchases

For In-App Purchases, the transaction process is handled securely by Apple's App Store. SpeedCube AR only receives a confirmation that a purchase was successful to unlock premium features. We do not have access to your credit card information or personal billing details.

Third-Party Services

SpeedCube AR does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media integrations that could compromise your privacy.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please feel free to reach out to us at speedcubesupport@gmail.com.
