Frequently Asked Questions

The AR scanner is not identifying my colors correctly.
Try to ensure you are in a well-lit room and that you are holding the cube steady. Avoid direct glare or shadows on the stickers.

Does this app support other types of puzzles?
Currently, SpeedCube AR is optimized specifically for the standard 3x3x3 cube. Support for other variants may be added in future updates!
