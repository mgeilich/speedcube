# App Store Release Notes: Version 2026.3.0

## What's New in SpeedCube AR

We've been hard at work to make your speedcubing journey even smoother! This update brings professional-grade solver enhancements and fixes to our learning tools.

### 🧩 NEW: Roux Method Solver
We are excited to introduce the **Roux Method Solver**! Known for its high move efficiency and intuitive block-building approach, you can now solve your cube using one of the most popular speedcubing methods in the world.
- **Move Efficiency**: Solutions are typically ~48 moves, significantly shorter than beginner methods.
- **Color Neutrality**: The solver automatically finds the most efficient block-building start regardless of your starting colors.
- **Advanced Moves**: Full support for M-slice (middle) rotations and wide moves.

### 🧩 CFOP Solver Upgrade: Advanced White Cross
For our advanced users, we've replaced the beginner "Daisy Method" with a move-optimized **Advanced White Cross** algorithm.
- **Direct Solving**: The solver now finds the most efficient, direct path to solve the white cross on the bottom face.
- **Optimized Move Count**: Most crosses are now solved in 5-8 moves, aligning with real-world CFOP techniques.

### 🎓 Layer-by-Layer Tutorial Refinements
We've polished our beginner tutorials to ensure a deterministic and frustration-free learning experience.
- **Level up the Yellow Cross**: Step 4 of the LBL guide has been refactored to consistently start from a true "dot" state, guiding you clearly through each orientation stage.
- **Orientation Correction**: We've standardized the orientation for Step 6 (Permutation) to use the correct Yellow-on-Top / White-on-Bottom perspective across all demos.

### ⚡ Performance & Polish
- Fixed a regression where some tutorial demos would start in an already-solved state.
- Improved the overall stability and move-sequencing of our AR-ready solvers.
- Cleaned up internal code and improved diagnostic logging for a smoother user experience.
